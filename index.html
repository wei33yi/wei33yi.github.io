Easy Thai Red Curry

  • Time: 40 mins
  • Serves: 4
IngredientsQuantity IngredientsQuantity
Skinless chicken breasts 1 1/2 lb Garlic 5 cloves
Shallot 2 Red curry paste 3 tablespoon
Ginger(freshly grated) 1 tablespoon Broccoli head 1-2
Lime juice(freshly squeezed) 2 tablespoons Green onions 2
Basmati rice 1 1/2 cups Cilantro(fresh chopped leaves) 3 tablespoons
Canola oil 1 tablespoon

Step 1: Cook rice according to package instructions; set aside.
Step 2: Cut skinless chicken breasts or thigh into 1-inch chuncks. Season with salt and pepper, to taste.
Step 3: Mince shallots and garlic. Thinly slice green onions. Cut broccoli into 3-inch pieces.
Step 4: Heat oil in a large stockpot or Dutch oven over medium heat. Add chicken, shallots and garlic to the stockpot and cook until golden, about 3-5 mins.
Step 5: Stir in red curry paste and ginger until fragrant, about 1-2 mins.
Step 6: Stir in coconut milk. Bring to a boil; reduce heat and cook, uncovered, stirring occasionally, unitl reduced and thickened, about 10-15 mins.
Step 7: Stir in broccoli until just tender, about 3 mins.
Step 8: Remove from heat; stir in green onions, cilantro and lime juice; seaon with salt and peper, to tase.
Step 9: Serve immediately with rice.

Source: Damn Delicious - Easy Thai Red Curry

Shephred's Beef Pie

  • Time: 90 mins
  • Serves: 6
IngredientsQuantity IngredientsQuantity
Onions2 Carrots2
Celery sticks4 Ground beef1 1/2 lb
Veg or other stock1 1/2cup Red wine1/2 cup
Potatos(medium size)3-4 Butter1 tablespoon
Tomato paste1/2 cup Chedder cheese(shredded)1 cup

Step 1: Chop the onions, carrots and celery.
Step 2: In a dutchoven over a low heat, cook the ground beef until juices start to run. Add the vegetables and cook for 15 mins until soften.
Step 3: Stir in the stock and wine and cook, covered, for 30 mins, stirring occasionally.
Step 4: Meanwile, cut the potatos into chuncks and boil in salted water for 10 mins. Drain well and stir in the butter.
Step 5: Stir the tomato paste into the meat, season with salt and pepper, to taste.
Step 6: Preheat oven to 400F.
Step 7: Spoon the potato peices on top of the meat, sprinkle over the cheese.
Step 8: Bake for 30 mins until golden.
Step 9: Serve immediately.

Modified from source: Good Food: Cheap Eats: Mediterranean Shephred's Pie, page 96.
